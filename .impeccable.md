## Design Context

### Users
Non-engineer and junior builders who shipped something fast with Lovable, Bolt, Cursor, or ChatGPT. They're proud of what they built and a little anxious about what they might have missed. They use this product on a laptop, usually in a browser, mid-afternoon. They want to feel like a professional caught their back — not like they're being scolded by a compliance tool.

### Brand Personality
Warm, calm, literate. "We caught what got missed." The tone is a knowledgeable friend, never alarmist. Three words: **careful · reassuring · precise**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark hero with animated SVG path texture, transitioning to a warm editorial minimal content section below. The hero is `#0e0c0b` (warm near-black, tinted slightly toward the brand crimson) with barely-visible flowing white paths as a texture layer — atmosphere, not decoration. Content sections below stay warm off-white (`#f7f5f2`), Instrument Serif for display and numerals, DM Sans weight 300 for body. One sharp accent: `#c0392b` (muted crimson) for risk and emphasis — used sparingly.

**The contrast between the dark hero and the warm editorial sections below is intentional and the defining visual moment.**

Borders are exactly 1px. No rounded corners anywhere — sharp and precise. Buttons use an ink-fill hover sweep (scaleX from left). No box-shadow heavier than `0 4px 24px rgba(17,16,16,0.06)`.

Anti-references: neon-on-dark SaaS dashboards, rounded glassmorphism cards, gradient text, ANY border-left accent stripes.

### Design Principles
1. **Dark hero, warm body.** The contrast between `#0e0c0b` and `#f7f5f2` is the signature moment. Don't flatten it.
2. **Paths as texture, not animation.** SVG paths in the hero are barely visible (opacity 0.03–0.06). They're atmosphere. Don't crank them up.
3. **Serif numerals as artifact.** Finding counts, stats, prices — always Instrument Serif. They feel typeset, not rendered.
4. **Motion serves narrative.** Entrance, state change, feedback — never decorative loops on content sections. The hero path animation is the sole exception.
5. **Red means something.** `#c0392b` is reserved for actual risk — critical findings, the italic "Is it secure?" hook. Overuse kills it.
6. **Sharp everywhere.** Zero border-radius on buttons, inputs, cards. No rounded-xl. The precision is the point.
